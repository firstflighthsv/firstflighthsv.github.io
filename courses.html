We offer hands-on piloting and design courses suitable for students of all ages and skill levels.

One or more Go Fly instructors are present at all events to assist students.

Piloting Series

The piloting series teaches students to fly and maintain RC aircraft. A variety of games such as golf, topple tower, and obstacle courses challenge students and teach fundamental piloting skills.

Design Series

Students learn about RC aircraft design and have the opportunity to build and fly their own DIY creations in the design series of courses. A must for RC aircraft mastery!
