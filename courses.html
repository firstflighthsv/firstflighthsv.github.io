We offer hands-on piloting and design workshops suitable for students of all ages and skill levels. We offer after-school programs, build workshops, and competitions.

One or more Go Fly instructors are present at all events to assist students.

Piloting Courses

The piloting series teaches students to fly and maintain RC aircraft. A variety of games such as golf, topple tower, and obstacle courses challenge students and teach fundamental piloting skills.

Design Courses

Students learn about RC aircraft design and have the opportunity to build and fly their own DIY creations in the design series of courses. Students walk away with a deep undestanding of RC aircraft and are well equipped to continue independently designing and building RC aircraft.
