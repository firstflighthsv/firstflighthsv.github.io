Go Fly is community centered organization that teaches flight courses and hosts community events related to RC aircraft.

Our mission is to inspire the next generation of local aerospace engineers, pilots, and designers. Our courses teach the basics while while encouraging exploration and creativity.

We offer hands-on courses to students in K-5, middle school, and high school, ranging from basic piloting and safety skills, to advanced level RC aircraft design workshops.

Programs include after-school events, day long workshops, and events.
